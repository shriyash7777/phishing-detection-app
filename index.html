Types of Phishing Attacks

📧

Email Phishing

Fake emails mimicking trusted brands to steal credentials

🎯

Spear Phishing

Targeted attacks using personal information about the victim

📱

Smishing

SMS-based phishing with fake bank alerts or OTP requests

📞

Vishing

Voice call phishing posing as bank officers or police

🐋

Whaling

High-value attacks targeting CEOs and senior executives

🔍

Search Engine

Fake websites appearing at top of search results via paid ads

🌐

Pharming

DNS poisoning to redirect users to fake websites

📋

Clone Phishing

Exact copy of legitimate email with malicious links

Stay Safe — Awareness Tips

Check the URL

Verify URL before entering credentials. Look for misspellings like paypa1.com

Look for HTTPS

Always check for the padlock icon. Never enter passwords on HTTP sites.

Verify Sender Email

Check if email domain matches the company. Fake: support@paypal.verify.com

Enable 2FA

Two-factor authentication protects account even if password is stolen.

Never Share OTP

No bank ever asks for OTP via phone call or email.

Report Phishing

Report to report@phishing.gov.in or forward to phishing@apwg.org
